AI
Definition: AI is a broad field concerned with creating machines and computer systems that can mimic human cognitive functions. Key Capabilities: These systems can perform tasks like: Learning: Acquiring knowledge and improving performance over time. Reasoning: Drawing logical conclusions and making inferences. Problem-solving: Identifying and resolving complex issues. Decision-making: Making choices based on available information. Perception: Understanding and interpreting sensory information (e.g., vision, speech). Language understanding: Comprehending and responding to human language. Underlying Technologies: AI relies on various technologies, including: Algorithms: Sets of instructions that enable computers to perform tasks. Machine learning: A type of AI that allows systems to learn from data without explicit programming. Deep learning: A subfield of machine learning that uses artificial neural networks to analyze data.
